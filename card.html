Cheetah

The survial of cheetahs are in peril with only 7,000 to 12,500 living in the wild :(

  • Scientific Name: Acinonyx jubatus
  • Average Length: 112 - 150 centimeters
  • Average Lifespan: 10 to 12 years
  • Habitat: Dry forests, scrub forests, and savannahs

The cheetah is a member of the cat family and is the fastest land animal. Being one of the most fearsome hunters on the planet, cheetahs can run at an astonishing speed of 112 km/h (70 mph) on short distances of 100 m (330 ft).
